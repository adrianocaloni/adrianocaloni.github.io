Ada Lovalece

Ada Lovelace, born in the early 19th century, was a mathematician and visionary thinker who left an indelible mark on the world of technology. Her pioneering work alongside Charles Babbage on the Analytical Engine, a groundbreaking mechanical computer, led her to write what is now considered the world's first computer program. In her notes on the Analytical Engine, Ada described an algorithm to calculate Bernoulli numbers, a complex mathematical task.
